Biography

I am an undergraduate physics student at the Institute of Science, Banaras Hindu University. My academic focus centers on advanced mathematical concepts, including vector calculus and numerical analysis, applied to real-world physical systems.

I specialize in 3D simulations and computational fluid dynamics, utilizing high-performance computing environments to model complex transport phenomena and acoustic properties.
