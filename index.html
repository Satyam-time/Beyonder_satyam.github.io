Applied Innovation

MEPPHS Mark II

Transitioning from theory to application, I focused on advanced multiphysics simulations. I developed and simulated a Venturi-based plasma reactor utilizing a multi-turn solenoid. This project heavily utilized COMSOL to optimize magnetic confinement and complex transport phenomena.

Hackathons & AI

GeoSage X & BEYONDER

Applying physics and logic to code, I developed GeoSage X for the ISRO Bharatiya Antariksh Hackathon—an autonomous geospatial thinker utilizing Chain-of-Thought LLMs.

At the IIT Delhi Hackathon, with Team BEYONDER, I conceptualized Docker-based APIs for an AI-powered cargo management system designed for extraterrestrial space stations.
